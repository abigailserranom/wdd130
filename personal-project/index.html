To Go or Not To Go

For Europeans, Norway is perhaps a staple in certain parts of the year like summer. But for people living on the American continent, Norway is unheard of. Despite being a very expensive country, depending on where you live, it is a country worth exploring. Scandinavians are not known for being the warmest people, but they are known for their atypical and astonishing architecture, environmental efforts, and Vikings. After spending a few days in Oslo and Bergen, I would go as far as to say that they should also be known for their artistry, cuisine, and complex simplicity. Find out where the yummy bites are and the attractions that served as pure therapy.
